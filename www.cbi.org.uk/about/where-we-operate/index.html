To ensure the voice of business is heard around the world and the CBI remains close to and responsive to its members wherever they are located, we have offices in 13 distinct geographical areas of the UK and offices in 5 distinct areas the world. For further information, simply hover or click the locations below.

In each area, the CBI has a director and a small team to ensure members have opportunities to be involved in consultations on policy and to hear from CBI policy experts, local MPs and ministers on how government action will affect them.

Around the UK

Our teams in Northern Ireland, Scotland and Wales also play a key role in representing the business voice in the devolved institutions. The CBI is uniquely placed to support business in these increasingly important arenas: our network of offices can draw on the expertise of more than 90 policy professionals at our London headquarters and our international bases to make the business case.

International

Successful business today is often synonymous with international growth, and the CBI provides its members with vital support to help them achieve their international ambitions including:

  • An opportunity to influence the international policy agenda on key global business issues.
  • Access to vibrant business-to-business (B2B) and business-to-government (B2G) networks in the UK and our overseas locations.
  • 'Soft' intelligence on key international markets.

Our offices in Washington DC, Beijing, New Delhi, Brussels and London work together to help CBI members prosper globally. Get in touch with our international team.
